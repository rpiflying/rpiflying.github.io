# Events & Meetings

The RPI Flying Club maintains an active schedule of weekly meetings and aviation events. [1]

### Weekly General Meetings
*   **When:** Wednesdays, 8:00 PM – 9:00 PM
*   **Where:** Phalanx Room (RU3502), Rensselaer Union

These meetings are open to all students across all majors. [1] Join us to discuss aviation, share stories, and plan upcoming trips to museums and air shows. [1]
